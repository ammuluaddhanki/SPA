FAQ's
Why Should I use Quick Lock?
Quick Lock allows you to lock your credit card from a mobile web app. If you cannot find one of your credit cards but are not sure it has been lost or stolen, you can use Quick Lock to lock your credit card while you look for it and potentially avoid fraudulent charges. If you are sure your card has been stolen or lost, lock your card and report it immediately.
What hanppens When I Lock my card?
When your card its locked it cannot be used to make new purchases, payments or cash withdrawal. However, all recurrent payments such as monthly subscriptions will still be processed.
Will recurring purchases (such as my Netflix subscription) continue if my card is locked?
Yes, all recurring purchases assigned to your credit card will continue to be charged when your card is locked
